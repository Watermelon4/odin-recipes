Water Chestnut Cake

Description

Water chestnut cake is a delicious Chinese dessert featuring the sweet and crisp water chestnut. Made with only a few ingredients and cooking in only 20 minutes, water chestnut cake is perfect for any occasion.

Recipe by Elena from chinasichuanfood.com

ingredients

  • 125 g water chestnut flour
  • 5 water chestnuts
  • 190 g sugar (any type)
  • 700 ml water
