Water Chestnut Cake

Description

Water chestnut cake is a delicious Chinese dessert featuring the sweet and crisp water chestnut. Made with only a few ingredients and cooking in only 20 minutes, water chestnut cake is perfect for any occasion.

Recipe by Elena from chinasichuanfood.com

Ingredients

  • 125 g water chestnut flour
  • 5 water chestnuts
  • 190 g sugar (any type)
  • 700 ml water

Directions

  1. Stir 150 ml water and the water chestnut flour together and wait 10 minutes to stir again.
  2. While waiting, add the rest of the water and sugar to a large pan on low heat. Wait until it begins to boil.
  3. Mince the water chestnuts and add them to the pan. Turn off the heat.
  4. When the pan begins boiling again, stir it into the other mixture and stir the completed mixture again.
  5. Steam the mixture for 20 minutes. You can test it by using a toothpick which will come out clean if it's done.
  6. Allow the cake to cool completely and serve. Optionally you can cool it in the fridge to serve cold or pan fry it to serve hot
